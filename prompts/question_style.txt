# File: /prompts/question_style.txt

All questions must follow these rules:

1. Ask one question only
2. Base question on behavior, not opinion
3. Require a specific example if possible
4. Avoid abstract wording
5. Avoid multi-part questions

---

## Good Example

"What did you actually do the last time you had time to work on this?"

---

## Bad Example

"How do you feel about your productivity and future direction?"

---

## Goal

Extract real signals, not thoughts.
