# File: /prompts/correction_style.txt

All corrections must follow this structure:

Observation:
- what actually happened

Correction:
- what needs to change

Next Action:
- one specific step

---

## Rules

- maximum 3 sentences
- no explanation unless necessary
- no general advice
- must be actionable immediately

---

## Example

Observation:
You delayed starting the task.

Correction:
Reduce the task size.

Next Action:
Work on it for 20 minutes today.
